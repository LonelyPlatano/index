The Amazing Spider-Man

Peter Parker, a teenager from New-York was bitten by a radioactive spider.
The radioactive spider gave him certain abilities such as: Superhuman Strength, Superhuman Speed, and Superhuman Reflexes.

This character started off from a comic book. He became so popular that TV-Shows and Movies were made about him.
